Shibata Lab

Department of Biomaterials and Engineering — Showa Medical University, Tokyo

Our department conducts cutting-edge research across dental materials, biomaterials, biomechanics, gene therapy, and biomedical engineering. From fundamental research to clinical application, we are committed to advancing dentistry and medicine through active international collaboration.

Research

Our Research Areas

🦷

Dental Materials & Biomaterials

Development and evaluation of dental adhesives, ceramics for restorative dentistry, and dental implant materials. We also conduct biomaterials research focused on tissue regeneration.

🔬

Biomechanics

Mechanical characterization of biological tissues, biomechanics of soft-hard tissue interfaces, cartilage and joint biomechanics, and the impact of abnormal extracellular matrix (ECM) mechanics on cell behavior in early disease onset.

🧬

Gene Therapy & Drug Development

Gene therapy research for osteoarthritis (OA) and osteoporosis (OP), and development of novel local bone formation methods using peptide drugs for alveolar ridge augmentation.

📊

Clinical & Translational Research

Clinical and translational research in hepatobiliary tumors, investigating the impact of extracellular matrix mechanical properties on the malignant behavior of tumor cells.

⚙️

Biomedical Engineering

Interdisciplinary research applying engineering approaches to dentistry and medicine, aiming to elucidate disease mechanisms and develop new diagnostic and therapeutic strategies.
